# Refund Policy

Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.

Duplicate charges are eligible for a full refund within 7 business days after verification.

Refunds are processed back to the original payment method.

Refund processing usually takes 5 to 10 business days.

Subscription renewals are refundable only if the customer contacts support within 48 hours of renewal.

Refunds are not available for accounts terminated due to policy violations.

Duplicate charges, double charges, or being charged twice are eligible for a full refund within 7 business days after verification.
