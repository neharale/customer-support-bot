    # Refund Policy

    Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.

    Refund requests made after 30 days are not eligible.

    Customers cannot get a refund after 30 days because refund requests are only eligible within 30 days of purchase.

    Refund processing time is a range. Since refunds usually take 5 to 10 business days, a refund may complete at any point within that range, but exact completion on a specific day is not guaranteed.

    Duplicate charges, double charges, or being charged twice are eligible for a full refund within 7 business days after verification.

    Refunds are processed back to the original payment method.

    Refund processing usually takes 5 to 10 business days.

    A refund may be completed within 10 business days, but it is not guaranteed.

    A refund is not guaranteed within 5 business days because normal processing takes 5 to 10 business days.

    Subscription renewals are refundable only if the customer contacts support within 48 hours of renewal.

    Refunds are not available for accounts terminated due to policy violations.
